To utilize this feature, follow these steps:

<Steps>
<Step title="Select multiple widgets of the same type (e.g., three Picture widgets) by holding the Shift key and clicking on each widget (or by clicking + selecting the area around them)."/>
<Step title="The widget attributes will appear on the right side of your screen."/>
<Step title="You can now edit various settings such as fonts, colors, radius, styles, animations, and more. Any changes made will be applied to all the selected widgets simultaneously."/>
</Steps>

<Info>
If you see the property 'Mixed' in the widget's attributes, it means that the selected widgets have different styles, font sizes, etc.
</Info>

Let's try out this feature with the Text widget:

<Steps>

<Step title="Select all the Text widgets by holding the Shift key and clicking on each widget."/>
